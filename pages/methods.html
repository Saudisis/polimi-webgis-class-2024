Methodology

Our methodology integrates multiple data sources and analytical techniques to assess landslide susceptibility comprehensively.

Workflow

Our approach includes a comprehensive four-step process: data preprocessing for susceptibility mapping, susceptibility map generation, data preprocessing for exposure assessment, and exposure assessment. This systematic methodology ensures precise and reliable results in our geographic information system (GIS) studies.

Data preprocessing for susceptibility mapping

Starting data and preliminary analysis

We initiated our project by collecting pertinent datasets and performing initial analyses. Starting from these foundational layers, we meticulously clipped them to focus solely on the area of interest. Ensuring coherence between the Extent and Pixel size across all layers was a critical step in our process.
